\section{Expected Outputs}

The empirical outputs will include LSOA/MSOA maps, property-archetype summaries, distributional plots, and intervention comparisons. A central output is the de-risking dividend:
\begin{equation}
\text{De-risking dividend} = \text{Fuel Price-at-Risk}_{\text{before}} - \text{Fuel Price-at-Risk}_{\text{after}}.
\end{equation}

The paper will also identify de-risking gaps: places and household-dwelling types where fuel price protection is most needed but low-carbon intervention is least accessible.
